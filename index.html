Thursday, April 3 — Arrival & Coffee Trail

Early morning arrival → settle in → coffee estate walk → sunset viewpoint

4:00 – 5:30 AM
🚌 Board KSRTC Volvo from Bangalore (Majestic)
Kempegowda Bus Station. Take the earliest Volvo — grab snacks or coffee at the bus station before boarding. The drive through the ghats as dawn breaks is stunning.
₹550–700/person 📍 Majestic Bus Stand 🎟️ Book KSRTC
8:30 – 9:30 AM
☕ Arrive Chikmagalur → Breakfast
Check into your stay, freshen up. Grab a South Indian breakfast nearby — idli, dosa, vada, filter coffee. Don't skip the local filter kaapi!
₹80–150/person 🥗 100% Veg Options 📍 Find Cafes
10:30 AM – 1:00 PM
🌿 Coffee Estate Walk — Baba Budangiri / Seethalayam
Take a guided walk through a coffee estate. Learn how coffee is grown, processed, and roasted. Many estates around Aldur offer free or paid guided tours. Gorgeous misty surroundings in April.
₹100–300/person 🌿 Nature Activity 📍 Coffee Estates
⚡ Alternative Option
  • Baba Budangiri — sacred hill, lovely trail, coffee country views
  • Manjarabad Fort (Hassan, 40 km) — star-shaped fort, great views
1:30 – 2:30 PM
🍽️ Lunch
Head to a local restaurant in Chikmagalur town. Try Malgudi Café or Hotel Udupi Krishna — budget-friendly, vegetarian-heavy South Indian meals.
₹150–250/person 🥗 Veg-Friendly 📍 Restaurants Map
3:30 – 6:00 PM
🌅 Hirekolale Lake & Sunset
Calm lake on the edge of town — beautiful for an evening walk or picnic. The golden hour here is stunning, framed by rolling coffee-covered hills. Easy, flat walk around the lake.
Free 📍 Hirekolale Lake
⚡ Alternative Sunset Spots
  • Bababudangiri Peak Viewpoint — if you're staying near that area
  • Chikmagalur Golf Course Road — scenic, great for walks
7:30 PM onwards

Early dinner — long trekking day tomorrow! Try Kaapi Katte or Café Coffeeland for a relaxed evening. Rest well.

02

Friday, April 4 — Peaks, Waterfalls & Adventure

Early start → Mullayanagiri trek → Hebbe Falls → evening chill

5:30 AM
🌄 Early Start — Drive to Mullayanagiri
Hire a shared cab or rent bikes. Drive up through the Sarpadhari route — one of the most dramatic mountain roads in Karnataka. Arrive at the base before sunrise if possible.
₹400–500/person shared cab 📍 Mullayanagiri
6:30 – 9:00 AM
🏔️ Mullayanagiri Trek — Highest Peak in Karnataka
1,930m above sea level. The trail to the summit takes about 30–45 mins. You'll be above the clouds! Temple at the top. Wear sturdy shoes, carry water and light snacks. Best views on a clear morning.
₹30 entry fee ⚡ Moderate Difficulty 📍 Trek Start Point
⚡ Alternative Treks (nearby)
  • Kudremukh Trek (40 km away) — UNESCO site, more challenging, prior permit needed
  • Kemmanagundi / Z Point — easier walk, stunning valley views
9:30 – 10:30 AM
☕ Post-Trek Breakfast at the Summit Viewpoint
Small stalls near the base serve hot chai, bread omelette, and biscuits. Perfect post-hike fuel while soaking in the views. Carry some snacks too for backup.
₹50–100/person 🥗 Chai & Snacks
11:00 AM – 2:30 PM
💧 Hebbe Falls — Hidden Waterfall in the Forest
20 km from Mullayanagiri area (via Kalasa Road). A two-tiered waterfall (168 ft) nestled deep in the Bhadra Reserve Forest. Requires a jeep ride into the forest (jeeps available at the gate), or a 4 km hike. Worth every step.
₹200–250/jeep (shared) 📍 Hebbe Falls
⚡ Other Waterfall Options
  • Jhari Falls — less crowded, near Mudigere
  • Kallathigiri Falls — seasonal, close to Mullayanagiri
  • Bhadra Dam & Reservoir — scenic boat rides available
3:00 – 4:00 PM
🍽️ Late Lunch
Pack a lunch from town (poha, rotis, fruit) or eat at a dhaba on the highway back. Budget-friendly and filling. Or head back to Chikmagalur for a proper sit-down meal.
₹100–200/person 🥗 Veg Options Available
5:00 – 7:00 PM
🛒 Chikmagalur Market Walk + Coffee Shopping
Walk through the local market. Buy freshly roasted coffee beans, spice powders, and local honey as souvenirs. Prices are much cheaper than Bangalore.
₹200–500/person (shopping) 📍 Market Area
8:00 PM
🍴 Group Dinner — Best Day Deserves Best Meal
Treat yourselves to a nicer dinner at Cafe Coffeeland or Peekay's. Share dishes, swap stories from the trek, and celebrate the trip!
₹300–450/person 🥗 Veg-Friendly 📍 Restaurants

🥗 Vegetarian Note

All restaurants above are either pure-veg or clearly separate veg options. Chikmagalur is a predominantly South Indian town — idli, dosa, sambar, and thalis are universally vegetarian and excellent. Your strict veg member will be very happy here!

💡 Money-Saving Hacks

  • Eat thali meals and South Indian breakfasts — full, delicious, and 50% cheaper than cafés
  • Split a shared cab for Mullayanagiri instead of 4 separate autos
  • Book Airbnb with kitchen access and buy groceries from the local market for one meal
  • Book KSRTC bus directly from ksrtc.in (skip Redbus markup)
  • Buy coffee directly from estate shops — 40–50% cheaper than Bangalore stores
  • Use Google Maps offline mode before you go — saves on data in hilly areas
